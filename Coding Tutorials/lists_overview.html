Why are Lists used in HTML?

  • Lists bring structure and readability to content. They make it easier to present items in a clear, organized way:
  • Unordered lists are great for items that are equally important or unrelated to sequence.
  • Ordered lists are necessary when the sequence or priority matters.
  • Description lists work best for defining or explaining terms and concepts.
